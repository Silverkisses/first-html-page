About

The only issue with being short, is other people's interpretation of the limitations.

Greetings! I am a 5 foot tall hispanic woman. Since a young age I have always had a passion for computers and technology. Unfortunately when growing up, females weren't as encouraged to enter the field. Post 2001 technology bubble many people were under the false impression technology and computer jobs are unstable. There was a lot of fear that the field had a short life span and would burn out much like the fireworks on the Fourth of July.
